Dresses/Costumes

Black and White Smooth Dress Front

Black and White Smooth Dress Back

This dress was designed and created by me. It is made of black stretch velvet and white stretch charmeuse. The leotard is made from fine, stretch mesh. The belt is made of a 3-inch elastic covered in white stretch spandex and I hand glued all 2000+ Swarovski Crystals on it. The braclets were also made by me and hand stoned. It was used for amateur smooth competitions and performances throughout the United States. To see it in action check out the videos section.

Photo by Elaine Lee Photography
